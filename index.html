Introduction & Motivation

The Challenge

Wearable devices continuously collect valuable health data (sleep patterns, activity levels) that critically affect health, yet this data remains underused in clinical tools and standard LLMs struggle to contextualize raw sensor metrics.

Opportunity

  • Continuous sensor data offers tailored health insights
  • 24/7 monitoring provides unprecedented detail
  • Personal patterns emerge over time

Our Solution: PH-LLM

  • Fine-tuned Gemini Ultra for health coaching
  • Integrates wearable sensor summaries
  • Provides personalized sleep & fitness advice
3/8

Methods & Model Architecture

Base Model: Gemini Ultra 1.0

Two-stage fine-tuning process to create PH-LLM

Stage 1
Text-Based Fine-tuning

Fine-tune full Gemini model on case-study Q&A and coding tasks to interpret sensor data

Stage 2
Multimodal Adapter

Train MLP adapter that encodes daily sensor summaries into LLM's latent space

Training Datasets

  • MCQs: Board exam-style questions in sleep medicine and fitness
  • Case Studies: 857 real-world cases with 15-30 days of wearable data
  • PRO Prediction: 4,163 users with ‚â•15 days of data and sleep surveys
